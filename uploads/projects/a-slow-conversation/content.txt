# A Slow Conversation

*An interactive generative sketchbook. You draw, it draws back.*

*[ preview images: 1.tonesunfinished.png, 2.tonesfinished.png, 3.tangles.png, 4.mandalas.png]*

Most generative art runs on its own. This one waits for you. You draw a mark and pause; it finds the shapes your lines have enclosed and quietly fills them with pattern, then waits for your next move. Draw, pause, watch it answer, draw again, an unhurried back-and-forth between a hand and a machine.

Built with vanilla Canvas2D, no framework, no build step.
